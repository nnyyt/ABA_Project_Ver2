La Veranda Hotel, Larnaca

Welcome to La Veranda de Larnaca, your exquisite haven for a perfect getaway! Nestled on a small hill just 5 minutes from Larnaca Airport, our charming hotel boasts 28 thoughtfully designed rooms that blend modern comfort with Mediterranean charm. Immerse yourself in the beauty of our surroundings, as we are conveniently situated within walking distance from the pristine sands of McKenzie Beach and just moments away from the vibrant heart of Larnaca city.

At La Veranda de Larnaca, we pride ourselves on offering a serene escape with easy access to both leisure and business destinations. Whether you're seeking relaxation by the sea or exploring the cultural delights of Larnaca, our hotel provides the ideal starting point for your adventures.
